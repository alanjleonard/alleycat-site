What

An Alleycat is like a bike race, but more like a scavenger hunt for locations in Hamilton.

When

Start time is at Noon on September 12th, 2021.

How to sign up

You don’t sign up.

How it works

  1. The race goes live at 12:00 Noon on Sunday September 12th.
  2. Then, go to the Race App (not live yet)
  3. You’ll see a list of 4 clues. For example:
    4. People stop and people GO, this Central location is a spot that commuters will know.
  4. Go to the clue location
  5. Find the race marker with the code. It will look like this
  6. Input the code into the Race App
  7. Answer all four questions to unlock the finish line clue
  8. Go to the finish line and type the last code into the app to get an official time

The start line

There isn’t a start line for this race. You can start from anywhere. The clues will become available online at exactly 12:00 noon on Sunday Sept 12th.

the course

The bounds for the clues and finish line will be down the Mountain, East of Dundurn, and West of Ottawa.

the finish

The finish line will be unlocked after you complete all four clues. To finish the race and get an official time, you have to type in the final code at the finish line.

the prizes

  • Spoke cards and bragging rights for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
  • Extra special Spoke Card for DFL (Dead Fucking Last)
  • Refreshments & good vibes at the finish line

the rules

  2. Have fun

  5. Individual or teams are allowed
  6. Ride safe
  7. The winner is the first person or team to input the code from the finish line into the app. Remember you can only do that after you’ve unlocked all four clues.

FAQ

  • Do I have to do the clues in order?

    No, you don’t have to do the clues in order, but you have to get them all to unlock the finish line.

  • What do I need to bring?
    • A charged smartphone with data
    • A fast, human-powered mode of transportation
    • A good mood & a bad attitude
  • Who’s behind all this?

    Try harder.

    Or Google it, they won’t be that hard.

  • What if I get there and can’t find the marker?
