Take back the streets

Once again we're taking to the streets of Hamilton. This time we're ripping thought the autumnal foliage the Sunday after Thanksgiving to find 4 more clues and unlock the finish line.

STEEL CITY ALLEYCAT II saw almost 100 riders on all different types of bikes. From E-bikes, tandem bikes, fixed-gear bikes, Sobi bikes, and even a recumbent bike. Over 350 clues entered into the race site with 68 teams or individuals finishing the race. The raddest riders got t-shirts, 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place got spoke cards.

STEEL CITY ALLEYCAT 3 will be the same format with 4 new clues and a new finish line.

What

An Alleycat is like a bike race, but more like a scavenger hunt for locations in Hamilton.

When

Start time is at Noon on October 16th, 2022.

How to sign up

You don’t sign up.

How it works

  1. Race goes live at 12:00 Noon on Sunday October 16th, 2022.
  3. You’ll see a list of 4 clues. For example:
    4. BANDSHELL
  4. Go to the clue location
  5. Find the race marker with the code. It will look like this:
  6. Input the code into the Race Site →
  7. Answer all four questions to unlock the finish line clue
  8. Go to the finish line and type the last code into the Race Site time

The start line

There isn’t a start line for this race. You can start from anywhere. The clues will become available on the Race Site → October 16th, 2022.

the course

The bounds for the clues and finish line will be down the Mountain, East of Dundurn, and West of Ottawa.

the finish

The finish line will be unlocked after you complete all four clues. To finish the race and get an official time, you have to type in the final code at the finish line.

the prizes

  • Spoke cards and bragging rights for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
  • Tshirts for the raddest riders

the rules

  1. Ride safe
  2. Have fun
  3. Friendly competition only. The prize is a fucking piece of paper, so relax.
  4. ONLY human powered travel is allowed.
  5. No Sharing Clues. You can ride as a team, but you can't split up. I'll know.
  6. If you're on a team, use only one phone.
  7. Ride safe.
  8. The winner is the first person or team to input the code from the finish line into the Race Site → after you’ve unlocked all four clues.

FAQ

  • Do I have to do the clues in order?

    No, you don’t have to do the clues in order, but you have to get them all to unlock the finish line.

  • What do I need to bring?
    • A charged smartphone with data
    • A fast bike
    • Good mood, Bad attitude
