What

An Alleycat is like a bike race, but more like a scavenger hunt for locations in Hamilton.

When

Start time is at Noon on September 12th, 2021.

How to sign up

You don’t sign up.

How it works

Visit https://race.steelcityalleycat.com at 12:00 noon on Saturday September 12th to see a list of 4 clues. An example clue would be:

People stop and people GO, this Central location is a spot that commuters will know.

The answer to this one is the Central GO station on Hunter St. So then you would ride there and find the Alleycat Marker which would look like this:

You would then type in the code into the app and that clue will be complete. After you find all four clues, the finish line clue will be revealed.

Ride to the finish line and type the last code into the app to get an official time.

You don’t have to do the clues in order. But you need to complete them all to unlock the finish line clue.

If two people arrive at the same time, the first person to type in the final code will win. The app will be the official timer.

The start line

There isn’t a start line for this race. You can start from anywhere. The clues will become available online at exactly 12:00 noon on Saturday June 19th.

the course

The bounds for the clues and finish line will be down the Mountain, East of Dundurn, and West of Ottawa.

the finish

The finish line will be unlocked after you complete all four clues. To finish the race and get an official time, you have to type in the final code at the finish line.

the prizes

  • Spoke cards and bragging rights for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.
  • Extra special Spoke Card for DFL (Dead Fucking Last).
  • Refreshments & good vibes at the finish line.
